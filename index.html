🤝『TEAM MEMBERS』

  • ⭐ Sudaim (9M1)
  • ⭐ Mithun Raj (9M1)

🔥『FEATURES』

  • 📱 Bluetooth Controlled
  • 🎙️ Voice Command Control
  • 🚧 Obstacle Avoidance
  • 🔄 Multi Mode Switching

🛠️ 『COMPONENTS』

  • Arduino UNO
  • Ultrasonic Sensor
  • Motor Driver L298N
  • DC Motors
  • Bluetooth HC-05
  • IR Sensors
  • Battery Pack
  • Chassis
  • Jumper Wires

🔧『HOW IT WORKS』

  • 🔋 Battery powers Arduino and motors
  • 🧠 Arduino reads commands and controls everything
  • ⚙️ Motor Driver moves the car in all directions
  • 📡 Bluetooth connects phone to car for controlling movement
  • 🎙️ Voice commands convert speech to actions like Forward or Stop
  • 📏 Ultrasonic sensor avoids obstacles automatically

✅『USES OF A REAL 3-IN-1 SMART CAR』🚗🤖

  • 📦 Transport items in schools, hospitals, or industries
  • 🚶 Accident prevention using obstacle sensors
  • 🗣️ Helps those who cannot use hands for control
  • 🏡 Home assistant for carrying small objects
  • 🏭 Automated load movement inside factories
  • 🚧 Enter risky or narrow areas safely
  • 🚀 Works like future self-driving technology
