🔧 How It Works

  • 🔋 Battery powers Arduino and motors
  • 🧠 Arduino reads commands and controls everything
  • ⚙️ Motor Driver moves the car in all directions
  • 📡 Bluetooth connects phone to car for controlling moves
  • 🎙️ Voice commands convert speech to actions like Forward or Stop
  • 📏 Ultrasonic sensor avoids obstacles smartly
